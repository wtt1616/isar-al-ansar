## How to Install

### For Android Phones (Samsung, Xiaomi, Huawei, etc.)

1. **Open Google Chrome** browser on your phone

2. **Visit** the website:
   ```
   https://isar.myopensoft.net
   ```

3. **Log in** with your email and password

4. **Look for** the install message at the bottom or top of screen:
   - It will say "Install iSAR" or "Add to Home screen"

5. **Tap "Install"** or **"Add"**

6. **Done!** The iSAR icon will appear on your home screen

**If you don't see the install message:**
- Tap the **3 dots (â‹®)** in the corner
- Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

---

### For iPhone/iPad (iOS)

1. **Open Safari** browser (must use Safari, not Chrome!)

2. **Visit** the website:
   ```
   https://isar.myopensoft.net
   ```

3. **Log in** with your email and password

4. **Tap the Share button** at the bottom center of screen
   - It looks like a square with an arrow pointing up: â–¡â†‘

5. **Scroll down** the menu that appears

6. **Tap "Add to Home Screen"**

7. **Tap "Add"** in the top right

8. **Done!** The iSAR icon will appear on your home screen

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to go to App Store/Play Store?
**A:** No! Install directly from the website using the steps above.

### Q: Does it cost money?
**A:** No, it's completely free.

### Q: Will it take up a lot of space?
**A:** No, very small - only a few MB.

### Q: Will it work offline?
**A:** Yes, you can view previously loaded schedules offline. But you need internet to load new schedules or submit availability.

### Q: How do I update it?
**A:** It updates automatically! Just use it normally.

### Q: Can I uninstall it?
**A:** Yes, just like any app - long press the icon and delete.

### Q: Why doesn't it work on my phone?
**A:** Make sure you're using:
- **Android:** Google Chrome browser
- **iPhone:** Safari browser (not Chrome)

### Q: I don't see the install button
**A:** Try:
- Refresh the page
- Log in first
- Use the menu (3 dots) > "Install app"

### Q: What if I have problems?
**A:** Contact your Head Imam or system administrator.

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't find install button

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you logged in first
2. Look at the top or bottom of screen
3. Try tapping menu (3 dots) > "Install app"
4. Refresh the page

### Problem: Icon disappeared from home screen

**Solution:**
- Just install again - your data is safe
- Your login and settings are saved

### Problem: App won't open

**Solution:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Try clearing browser cache
3. Uninstall and reinstall
4. Contact support

### Problem: Can't log in

**Solution:**
1. Check email and password are correct
2. Make sure you're connected to internet
3. Contact your Head Imam to reset password
